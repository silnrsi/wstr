## Script description

Han with Bopomofo is a combination of the [Han](/scrlang/script-hani) script and [Bopomofo](/scrlang/script-Bopo).

<details>
<summary>Read the full description...'</summary>Bopomofo is often used to annotate the pronounciation of Han characters. When the two scripts are used together they are called Han with Bopomofo.
</details>
