The Tai Dam language is written in three scripts. This facilitates reading by speakers from different educational backgrounds. As well as the Tai Viet script, a modified form of the [Lao script](/scrlang/scripts/laoo) and a modified form of the [Latin script](/scrlang/scripts/latn) as used for Vietnamese are also used. All three scripts represent the same sounds.
