## Script description

The Old Sogdian script encompasses a group of related scripts used to represent Sogdian, an ancient Eastern Iranian language.

<details>
<summary>Read the full description...</summary>Old Sogdian unifies the scripts used in short inscriptions on coins and vessels, the Ancient Letters and the Kultobe and Upper Indus inscriptions.

Old Sogdian contains 40 characters: 29 letters, 10 numbers, 1 heterogram.

The ordering of numbers follows the right-to-left directionality of the script.
</details>
