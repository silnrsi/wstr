In The Unicode Standard, implementation of Egyptian Hieroglyphs is discussed in {link:http://www.unicode.org/versions/latest/ch11.pdf Chapter 11 Cuneiform and Hieroglyphs}.

[comment]: # (end of intro)

[comment]: # (start of blocks)

The Egyptian Hieroglyphs were encoded in The Unicode Standard version 5.2. The script is encoded in the following blocks:

table:
Blocks | Character Range | Added in Unicode Version | Unicode Chart |
Egyptian Hieroglyphs | 13000..1342F | 5.2 | {link:http://www.unicode.org/charts/PDF/U13000.pdf U13000 |
Egyptian Hieroglyph Format Controls | 13430..1343F | 12.0 | {link:https://www.unicode.org/charts/PDF/U13430.pdf U13430 |

[comment]: # (end of blocks)

[comment]: # (start of chars)
