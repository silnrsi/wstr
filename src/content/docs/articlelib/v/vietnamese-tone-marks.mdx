These two characters were apparently originally added for Vietnamese use. However, they are now discouraged from use:

- :usv[0340]{usv} :usv[0300]{char} :usv[0340]{name}
- :usv[0341]{usv} :usv[0301]{char} :usv[0341]{name}


Instead, you should use:

- :usv[0300]{usv char name}
- :usv[0301]{usv char name}
