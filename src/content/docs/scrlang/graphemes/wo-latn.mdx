This information is based on the current orthography. It does not include obsolete characters.

In Wolof, the letter q is equivalent to x after a nasal (xonx = xonq) or at a geminate xx (ñaxx -ñaq)

Geminated consonants are documented because they are significant in the Arabic script orthography.

Sources:

- Hartell, Rhonda. 1993. _Alphabets of Africa._ Dakar, Senegal. UNESCO and Summer Institute of Linguistics.
