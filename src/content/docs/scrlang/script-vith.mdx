<summary>Read the full description...'</summary>It is sometimes called Veqilharxhi, after its inventor, Naum Veqilharxhi, or Vithkuqi, after his birthplace, the village of Vithkuq. The script was written from left to right using thirty-two characters. It is no longer used.

_This script is not currently recognized by the [ISO 15924 standard](http://www.unicode.org/iso15924/), but is included in ScriptSource for research purposes.
