These data come from words collected in Flex (not yet published) by Ca̱hungwa̱rya̱ Language Project (CLP).

Ca̱hungwa̱rya̱ [nat] has 5 different dialects, Makangara dialect is the reference dialect, chosen by the community leaders and representatives.

Although Ca̱hungwa̱rya̱ is a tonal language, writing tone has yet to be tested. Probably grammatical tone should be indicated.
