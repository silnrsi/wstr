Source:[http://www.silcam.org/documents/AlphabetandOrthographyStatementforFulfuldeFUBAjamiyafortheinternet.pdf]

In the context of shadda + kasra, this language leaves the kasra below the consonant rather than following the Arabic practice of moving the kasra below the shadda.
