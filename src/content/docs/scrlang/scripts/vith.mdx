## Script description

Büthakukye was an alphabetic script invented around 1840 for writing the Albanian language.

<details>
<summary>Read the full description...</summary>It is sometimes called Veqilharxhi, after its inventor, Naum Veqilharxhi, or Vithkuqi, after his birthplace, the village of Vithkuq. The script was written from left to right using thirty-two characters. There is modern interest in this historic script.
</details>
